## Overview

A full-featured e-commerce platform designed to provide a seamless shopping experience for both customers and merchants. The platform includes advanced features like real-time inventory management, secure payment processing, and comprehensive analytics.

## Key Features

- **Product Management**: Advanced product catalog with categories, variants, and inventory tracking.
- **Shopping Cart**: Persistent shopping cart with real-time price updates and tax calculations.
- **Payment Integration**: Secure payment processing with multiple payment gateways including Stripe and PayPal.
- **Order Management**: Complete order lifecycle management from placement to fulfillment.
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Comprehensive analytics for sales, customer behavior, and inventory performance.
- **Mobile Responsive**: Fully responsive design that works seamlessly across all devices.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js**: For server-side rendering and optimal performance.
- **Node.js**: For backend API development and server-side logic.
- **PostgreSQL**: For robust data storage and complex queries.
- **Stripe API**: For secure payment processing.
- **Redis**: For caching and session management.
- **Docker**: For containerized deployment and scalability.

## Challenges and Learnings

The biggest challenge was handling high concurrent traffic during peak shopping periods while maintaining fast response times. This required implementing efficient caching strategies, database optimization, and load balancing. Additionally, ensuring secure payment processing and PCI compliance was crucial and required careful attention to security best practices.

## Outcome

The e-commerce platform has successfully processed thousands of transactions and provided a smooth shopping experience for customers. The platform's scalability and performance have been proven during high-traffic events, and the comprehensive analytics have helped merchants optimize their business operations.
